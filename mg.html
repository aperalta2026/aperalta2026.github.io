This is McGonagall, or McGee for short. He's the first cat we took into the family. He came home to us on Christmas of 2020.

McGee is a Russian Blue. His birthday is on September 15 and he just turned 2 this year.
McGee's a really cuddly cat. You start petting him and he starts purring within seconds.

We've had McGee the longest out of all our cats, but we still don't know what his favorite food is. He's a really picky eater. One thing we do know for sure, though, is that he doesn't like fish. Quite out of character for a cat.
He likes to act tough in front of other cats. He gets into fights with strays often and growls at Aiko and Tofu when they're eating together.

But when it comes to humans, especially my dad, he softens up and acts like a baby. He makes his meow really high pitched and comes to us for pets. It's funny to watch.
