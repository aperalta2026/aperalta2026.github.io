This is Aiko, our second cat. She's a Russian Blue as well. We brought her home in September 2021, when she was only around 3 months old. She's a little over a year old now.

We aren't really sure when her exact birthday is, but we assume its near June 2021.
Aiko isn't really the affectionate type. Ever since she was little, she would try to avoid our hands when we try to pet her. The most she'd do to show her "affection" is sit/lay down next to you.
She may look a bit young in the photos, but Aiko is a mother now. Together with McGee, she had Tofu and her 2 siblings, as well as a new batch of kittens just recently.
